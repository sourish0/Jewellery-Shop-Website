Credit Card Payments: At Sitanath Jewellers, we strive to provide a seamless shopping experience, and our diverse payment options reflect our commitment to convenience. You can effortlessly complete your purchase using major credit cards, ensuring a secure and efficient transaction.

Cash Transactions: For those who prefer the traditional route, Sitanath Jewellers gladly accepts cash payments. We understand the significance of choice in payment methods and aim to accommodate your preferences while maintaining the trust and reliability associated with our brand.

Digital Wallets (PhonePe and Paytm): Embracing the digital age, we offer the convenience of digital wallet payments through platforms like PhonePe and Paytm. Experience swift and hassle-free transactions with the tap of your smartphone, adding an extra layer of ease to your shopping journey.

SBI Online: As part of our commitment to providing a variety of payment options, we accept transactions through SBI Online. Your secure and seamless payment experience is our priority, ensuring that you can complete your purchase with confidence.

Mastercard: Sitanath Jewellers welcomes Mastercard holders for a convenient and globally recognized payment solution. Enjoy the flexibility and security that comes with using your Mastercard to make your jewelry selections with us.

UPI (Unified Payments Interface): Keeping up with the latest in digital payments, we support transactions through UPI. Experience the ease of transferring funds directly from your bank account, providing you with a modern and efficient payment alternative.

At Sitanath Jewellers, our commitment to a diverse and secure payment process reflects our dedication to meeting the unique needs of our valued customers. Choose the option that suits you best and enjoy a worry-free transaction every time you shop with us.

Established in 1980 by the visionary jeweler, the late Sitanath Chandra, Sitanath Jewellers stands as a beacon of opulence and artistic brilliance. The shop has seamlessly evolved into a distinguished establishment under the capable stewardship of his successor, Somnath Chandra. As the torchbearer of the family legacy, Somnath has skillfully woven innovation into the rich tapestry of tradition, ensuring each piece not only boasts exquisite craftsmanship but also narrates a profound story.
The ornate façade of our establishment, inviting patrons into a realm where passion meets precision, reflects the commitment to Sitanath Chandra's founding principles of integrity and quality. Sitanath Jewellers is more than a retail space; it is an immersive experience where patrons embark on a journey through time, encapsulating the dreams of a founder who envisioned timeless elegance. The remarkable legacy of Sitanath Chandra, initiated in 1980, lives on through the brilliance of every jewel and the enduring reputation of Sitanath Jewellers.
